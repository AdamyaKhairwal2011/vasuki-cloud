Our Approach

How we teach differently

No rote memorization
Every concept is taught by breaking it — students are given a broken system and must debug it before understanding it.
Small cohorts only
Maximum 18 students per cohort. Every student gets a named engineer as a mentor, not just access to a Slack channel.
Real infrastructure
Labs run on actual Vasuki Cloud infrastructure — not toy sandboxes. By the end you've shipped to a real server.
Industry-led sessions
Guest sessions from CTOs and senior engineers at Indian startups. Not career advice — actual technical war stories.

FAQ

Common questions

Do I need prior programming experience?
No prior experience is needed for the Foundation track. We start from logical thinking before writing a single line of code. If you already know the basics, you'll move quickly through Phase 1 and get more from Phases 2 and 3.
Is it really free?
Yes. Vasuki Learning is completely free for all three tracks. We believe the talent gap in Indian tech is a distribution problem, not a willingness problem. Our costs are covered by Vasuki iTech's infrastructure revenue.
What do I get at the end?
A Vasuki Learning certificate, a live project hosted on Vasuki Cloud infrastructure, a code review report from our engineers, and direct access to our alumni network. We also share standout graduates with our network of partner companies.
How large are the cohorts?
Maximum 18 students per cohort for the live tracks. We deliberately keep them small so every student gets real attention. Applications are competitive — we review each one personally.
Is this connected to Vasuki Cloud / Vasuki iTech?
Yes. Vasuki Learning is a product of Vasuki iTech. Labs run on real Vasuki Cloud infrastructure. Instructors are active engineers at the company. This isn't a side project — it's how we grow the ecosystem of builders who use our tools.
