About Us

We build technology
that works for you.

Vasuki iTech Labs is a focused, two-person technology studio. We help businesses and institutions solve real problems with software — without the overhead of a large agency or the risk of an untested freelancer.

The Studio

Founded with a simple premise: good technology should be accessible to every business, not just the ones that can afford a full engineering department. We work closely with our clients — understanding the problem first, building second — and we take responsibility for the outcome.

Our work spans AI automation, web development, cloud infrastructure, and rapid product development. Every engagement is hands-on. Every deliverable is production-ready.
