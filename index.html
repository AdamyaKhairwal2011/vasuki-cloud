How It Works

From namespace to production in three moves.

01
Claim a Namespace

Reserve your unique cloud handle during onboarding. This handle anchors your team's identity across every Vasuki product and sub-service.

02
Connect Your Tools

Integrate Guppt, Argon, Vaimanika, Forms, or Vypaar via a single unified API key. Zero separate credentials. No per-product onboarding loops.
